🐝 What is VarroaMate Productivity?

VarroaMate Productivity (VMP) is a companion app to VarroaMate, designed to help beekeepers track hive performance and productivity over the years — not just health data in isolation.

Where VarroaMate focuses on Varroa mite monitoring, treatments, and biosecurity reporting, VMP adds a longer view: harvests by season, queen histories, detailed inspections, colony losses, and productivity insights that connect mite control outcomes with honey yield.

📊 What VMP tracks

  • Honey harvest — yield per hive, per season, year on year
  • Inspections — glove-friendly field notes with voice entry, synced with mite count data from VarroaMate
  • Queen register — age, origin, race, status, and replacement signals across your whole apiary
  • Colony losses — dead-outs and abscondments with cause tracking, including notifiable disease flags
  • Insights — apiary health at a glance, yield vs mite pressure, system comparisons, and loss analysis

💡 Built for real practice

Every beekeeper's practice is different. VMP is designed to surface patterns specific to your operation — which hives perform best, which treatments correlate with better outcomes, when colony losses peak, and what's working across your apiaries over time.

The more consistently you record, the more useful the insights become. A few seasons of data transforms a log into a genuine management tool.

ℹ️ How inspections & mite counts sync
▾
📱
VarroaMate (VM) is the source of truth for mite counts. Counts entered via the green field button or red regular entry button in VM are stored in the mite_counts table and appear here as 🔬 3/300 pills on the matching inspection.
🔍
VarroaMate Productivity (VMP) adds a Mite Count section to the inspection modal. If you enter a count here, it is written directly to VM's mite_counts table and becomes visible in VM immediately — no duplication, same data.
🏷️
Entries made in VMP are tagged with a VMP badge so you can tell them apart from VM entries at a glance.
🔗
A mite count pill appears on an inspection when a count exists for the same hive within ±1 day of the inspection date — whether that count was entered in VM or VMP.
⚠️
AFB and EFB are nationally notifiable pests. If you select these in the disease observations, VMP will show a biosecurity alert with a link to your state authority. Always report positive detections promptly.
