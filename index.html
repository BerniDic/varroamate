About Us

We are Nena and Bernhard, a hobby beekeeper couple from the Yarra Valley who share a deep enthusiasm for bees and the wonderful community of people who care for them. Beekeeping has always been more than a hobby for us. It is a constant source of curiosity, learning, and connection with others who love bees as much as we do.

Like many beekeepers, we have been thinking a lot about how the arrival of Varroa will change beekeeping in Australia. We saw that many hobbyists feel uncertain and worried about how to manage their hives once Varroa becomes part of everyday beekeeping. At the same time, new technologies such as artificial intelligence offer exciting opportunities to make complex information easier to understand and apply.

This is why we created VarroaMate. Our goal is simple. We want to support beekeepers with practical tools, clear guidance, and confidence as they navigate beekeeping in the age of Varroa. VarroaMate is built by beekeepers, for beekeepers, with the hope that it helps the community continue to thrive while keeping strong and healthy colonies. 🐝
